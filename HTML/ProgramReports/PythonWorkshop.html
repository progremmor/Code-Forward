Why Python? A survey of past workshop participants ranked Python as the #1 topic of interest, reaffirming its relevance in today’s tech-driven world. Our Python Coding Workshop was a fantastic success! Students came ready to dive into one of today’s most in-demand programming languages—and they didn’t disappoint.

This beginner-friendly session covered the fundamentals of Python, including variables, loops, conditionals, and practical problem-solving techniques. Through hands-on exercises and three mini-projects, participants gained the confidence to write their first lines of Python code and beyond.

The workshop attracted a diverse group of learners, over 80% of whom had little or no prior coding experience. Feedback was overwhelmingly positive, highlighting the approachable teaching style, interactive format, and supportive learning environment that made coding feel accessible and fun.

Over the course of the workshop, participants learned the fundamentals of Python programming, including:

  • Variables and Data Types

  • Loops and Conditionals

  • Functions

  • Lists and Dictionaries
