Summer Camp: Code Forward x Lexington Community Center Collaboration

July 2026

We are thrilled to partner with Lexington Recreation & Community Programs to offer a FREE 2-week online Summer Coding Program for students interested in learning coding and exploring technology in a fun and engaging environment!

Whether you're new to coding or looking to build on your existing skills, this program will provide hands-on learning, creative projects, and a chance to discover the exciting world of computer science.

📅 Free 2-Week Online Program
💻 Learn Coding Skills
🎉 Interactive Activities & Projects

A big thank you to Lexington Recreation & Community Programs for helping us make STEM education more accessible to our community. We can't wait to learn and create together this summer!
