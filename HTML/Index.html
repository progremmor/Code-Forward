Our Mission

CodeForward is an educational non-profit organization that aims to teach technology and empower students from all backgrounds including under-served communities by providing accessible, high-quality computer science, game design, and coding tutoring. We are located in Lexington, MA. and we believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore, learn, and thrive in the digital world. Through mentorship, hands-on learning, and community partnerships, we aim to close the opportunity gap in tech education and inspire the next generation of diverse innovators.
