Get involved with CodeForward?

Whether you're a student exploring your first lines of code, a teacher inspiring the next generation, or a parent cheering from the sidelines, there's a place for you here. If you care deeply about expanding access to tech education and believe in the power of innovation to uplift communities, CodeForward welcomes you with open arms.



How to Help?

Volunteer

Are you bright, enthusiastic, and eager to make a difference? Volunteering with CodeForward is a fantastic way to gain hands-on experience in the nonprofit world. Whether you're leading coding workshops, mentoring younger students, or helping organize events, you'll be part of a dynamic team working to expand access to tech education. It's a challenge, but one that's incredibly rewarding.

Donate

CodeForward is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization powered by public donations. Every contribution, big or small, helps us bring accessible tech education to students from all backgrounds. If you believe in creating opportunities through technology, donating is quick, easy, and deeply impactful.

Events

From coding workshops to tutoring sessions, CodeForward is always hosting events that bring us closer to our mission: making tech education accessible to all. We invite you to be part of this journey, whether by volunteering, attending, or helping us grow!


Want to join us as a volunteer? Have an idea for an event? Have a question or concern? Contact us today!
